OUR CORE PRINCIPLE

Immersive Mastery

Building the most visual, responsive, and robust typing interface on the internet.

Entering the Flow State

SpeedType Studio was conceptualized as a solution to boring, standard word inputs. We believe that typing is more than just raw speed—it is a cognitive rhythm, a flow state where the boundary between computer and brain dissolves. By building highly visually-responsive tools (Three.js fluid backdrops, interactive key overlays, and lifetime dashboards), we help you achieve keyboard autonomy faster.

METRICS DEFINED
  • WPM: (Total characters / 5) / elapsed minutes
  • Accuracy: Percentage of exact matches over entered letters
  • Key Stability: Deviation cycle of keystroke typing gaps
